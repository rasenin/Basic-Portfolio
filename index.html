About Me

My name is Roman and I'm 21 years old. I'm currently studying Computer Science as an undergrad at UC Davis.

As a first-generation immigrant from Ukraine, life wasn't easy from the get-go but my family taught me the value of hard work and determination and I never took that for granted. As a kid I had my hobbies and pastimes, mostly music and sports. Technology was only starting to become widespread and for the majority of my childhood my family only had one computer. Nevertheless, that old hunk of silicon sitting there in our living room perked my interest into technology.

I always dreamed about someday learning how to create websites and applications, delving into the details of what makes modern computing so ubiquitous and life-transforming. This portfolio is part of that dream becoming reality and I'm super excited to now have a formal webpage I could call my very own.
